StartCam

WebRTC Video Streaming Over VPN

Phone

Streamer

Start streaming video from your phone camera. Use this on the device you want to stream from.
PC

Viewer

Watch the video stream on your PC or another device. Receives video from the streamer.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Open Streamer on your phone
  • Step 2: Open Viewer on your PC
  • Step 3: Start camera on phone and connect
  • Step 4: Enjoy real-time video streaming!
